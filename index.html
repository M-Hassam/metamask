What is SURF Reward?

SURF Reward offers an extension for all common browsers. This removes a large part of the advertising on the Internet and additionally exchanges individual advertising spaces with its own advertising partners. From now on, the profit will no longer go to the webmaster, but to you!
02
How it works

Why should I invest?

SURF Reward uses a large part of the advertising revenue for its liquidity pool and thereby ensures a permanent increase in the value of the SURF token.

This makes the SURF token lucrative for everyone. Whether you are an investor, advertising partner, pre-sale buyer, Hodler or a SURF Reward Extension user.

About us

SURF Reward is a new project from Abelius Capital AG.

Abelius Capital AG is a public limited company incorporated under German law in 2019 with its registered office in Düsseldorf, Germany. So far, the focus has been on investing in global and large-scale real estate projects. SURF Reward is their new (blockchain)project and they have put together a large and strong team for this purpose. All employees are on site, which enables direct communication and cooperation.

"List of benefits is long"

SURF Reward makes advertising on websites interesting again. Users are no longer automatically hide ads, but are demonstrably gaining interest. In addition, we reach target groups who frequently use ad-blockers and are more difficult to reach for certain products. Restrictions on the part of the providers can be circumvented. In many countries, advertising related to certain topics is prohibited, and completely new paths are opening up here. Surf Reward is not just a product, Surf Reward is innovation!
